PART III — NARRATIVE

State below in reasonable detail why Forms 10-K, 20-F, 11-K, 10-Q, 10-D, N-SAR, N-CSR, or the transition report or portion thereof, could not be filed within the prescribed time period.

Equinox Frontier Funds (the “Trust”) was unable to file its Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the period ended June 30, 2016 (the “Form 10-Q”) by the prescribed filing deadline of 5:30 pm EDT on August 15, 2016 without unreasonable effort and expense. The delay in filing the Form 10-Q resulted from delays in compiling certain necessary information to complete the closing procedures for the Form 10-Q and to prepare a complete filing of the Form 10-Q prior to the 5:30 pm EDT filing deadline.

Specifically, the Trust performed additional procedures in connection with the quarter ended June 30, 2016 in light of (1) significant structural changes to the Trust due to the transition to the Galaxy Plus Platform, an unaffiliated, third-party managed account platform and (2) the ongoing transition of Trust administration services and financial reporting from BNP Paribas Financial Services, LLC to Gemini Hedge Fund Services, LLC.
