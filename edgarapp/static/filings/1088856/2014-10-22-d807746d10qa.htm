EXPLANATORY NOTE

Corcept Therapeutics Incorporated, or the “Company”, is filing this amendment (the “Form 10-Q/A”) to our Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the quarter ended June 30, 2014 (the “Form 10-Q”), filed with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission on August 8, 2014, solely to amend Item 6 Exhibit Index of the Form 10-Q and re-file the agreement identified as Exhibit 10.2 to the Form 10-Q. The Company has made no further changes to the Form 10-Q.

This Form 10-Q/A should be read in conjunction with the original Form 10-Q, which continues to speak as of the date of the Form 10-Q. Except as specifically noted above, this Form 10-Q/A does not modify or update disclosures in the Form 10-Q. Accordingly, this Form 10-Q/A does not reflect events occurring after the filing of the Form 10-Q or modify or update any related or other disclosures.
